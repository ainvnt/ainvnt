BI & Analytics FAQ

Common questions about business intelligence and data analytics.

How long does BI implementation take

Simple implementations take 2-4 months. Complex enterprise projects take 6-12 months. We deliver value incrementally with phased dashboards, so you see ROI quickly.

Which BI tool should we choose

It depends on your data volume, budget, and team skills. Tableau excels at dashboards. Power BI offers Microsoft integration. Looker provides data modeling. We recommend the best fit for your use case.

What about data quality and governance

Critical. We establish data governance, master data management, and quality checks to ensure dashboards show reliable insights. Bad data = bad decisions.

Can we scale to handle real-time data

Absolutely. We design scalable architectures with streaming platforms (Kafka, Flink) for real-time dashboards that update by the second, not by the day.

How to ensure dashboard adoption

Change management, user training, and involving stakeholders early. Beautiful dashboards + executive sponsorship + ongoing support = high adoption rates.
