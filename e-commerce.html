Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about launching and growing your online store.

How long does it take to launch an e-commerce store

Timeline varies based on platform and complexity. Simple stores launch in 4-8 weeks, while enterprise platforms with integrations may take 3-6 months. We provide detailed project timelines upfront.

Which platform is best for our business

Platform selection depends on your business model, scale, and budget. We analyze your requirements and recommend the right platform (Shopify for SMBs, Magento for enterprise, custom solutions for unique needs).

How do you ensure security and PCI compliance

We implement PCI DSS Level 1 compliance, SSL/TLS encryption, tokenization, fraud prevention systems, and regular security audits to protect customer data and payment information.

Can you help with SEO for our store

Yes! We implement technical SEO, structured data markup, mobile optimization, and build SEO-friendly site architecture to improve search visibility and organic traffic.

What is included in post-launch support

We provide ongoing maintenance, performance monitoring, security updates, feature enhancements, analytics reporting, and strategic optimization recommendations.

How do you measure e-commerce success

We track key metrics: conversion rate, average order value, customer lifetime value, cart abandonment, bounce rate, and ROI. Monthly reporting keeps you informed of performance and opportunities.
