Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to help you move faster.

How quickly can a team evaluate fit?

That depends on the workflow and integrations involved, but the initial goal is to understand whether there is a strong fit for a focused pilot.

Is Ainvnt a product or a custom services offering?

Ainvnt is being positioned as a product platform. Conversations are centered on workflow fit, pilot scope, and platform evolution rather than a generic services menu.

What kinds of teams is Ainvnt for?

The strongest fit is for operations-heavy teams that manage document flows, approvals, internal requests, or exception-prone manual processes.

What is discussed in the first call?

We focus on the workflow itself: what triggers it, who reviews it, what systems are touched, and what a successful first rollout should look like.
