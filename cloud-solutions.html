Cloud Solutions FAQ

Answers to common questions about cloud migration and management.

How much will cloud migration cost

Migration costs depend on infrastructure size, complexity, and data volume. However, cloud typically saves 30-50% on operational costs within 12 months. We provide detailed ROI analysis during assessment.

Will there be downtime during migration

No. We use zero-downtime migration strategies including phased cutover, load balancing, and failover mechanisms. Your services remain operational throughout.

Which cloud platform is best for us

It depends on your workloads, budget, and requirements. We're multi-cloud experts (AWS, Azure, GCP) and recommend the best fit based on your specific use case and business goals.

How secure is cloud infrastructure

Cloud providers have enterprise-grade security. We add additional layers: encryption, IAM policies, security groups, VPCs, firewalls, and compliance frameworks (HIPAA, PCI-DSS, SOC 2).

What if we need to scale during peak season

Cloud auto-scaling grows with your demand automatically. Set up load balancers and auto-scaling policies, and your infrastructure expands seamlessly during peaks, shrinks during lowsâ€”you pay only for what you use.

Do you provide disaster recovery

Yes. We design and implement multi-region disaster recovery with automated failover, regular backups, and RTO/RPO targets aligned with your business requirements.
