Overview

What this policy covers

This privacy policy applies to information collected through the Ainvnt website, direct communications with our team, and service-related interactions that begin from this website. It does not automatically govern third-party platforms or client-owned systems unless a separate agreement says otherwise.

Contact details

Name, email, phone, company, and project information you submit.

Usage signals

Basic analytics, browser data, and page interactions used to improve site performance.

No sale of data

We do not sell personal information collected through this website.

Rights support

You can ask for access, correction, or deletion where applicable.

1. Information we collect

Information you choose to share and limited technical data

We may collect personal information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, company name, job title, and any details you include in a message, quote request, consultation booking, or support inquiry.

We may also collect limited technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring pages, approximate location derived from IP data, and website activity signals that help us understand performance and visitor behavior.

  • Inquiry and contact form submissions
  • Email, phone, or messaging conversations with our team
  • Scheduling or consultation request details
  • Website analytics and cookie-generated usage information

2. How we use information

Operational, service, and communication purposes

We use information to respond to inquiries, evaluate project needs, deliver requested services, operate our website, improve user experience, maintain security, and communicate about relevant service activity.

Respond and deliver

Answer questions, provide estimates, schedule meetings, and perform contracted work.

Improve the site

Measure traffic, diagnose technical issues, and refine pages and conversion flows.

Protect operations

Monitor abuse, troubleshoot incidents, and support compliance and recordkeeping needs.

3. Cookies and analytics

Cookies help us understand how the website is used

Our website may use cookies, local storage, and analytics tools to support site functionality, understand traffic patterns, and improve performance. These technologies may remember preferences, identify returning visits, and provide aggregated reporting.

You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. If you disable certain cookies, some features or user experience elements may not function as intended.

4. When information is shared

Shared only where needed for operations or legal reasons

We may share information with trusted service providers that support hosting, analytics, communications, scheduling, or technical operations, but only to the extent reasonably necessary for those functions.

We may also disclose information if required to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, enforce agreements, protect rights, or investigate suspected misuse of the website or services.

5. Data retention

We keep information only as long as it remains useful or necessary

Information is retained for as long as reasonably needed to respond to inquiries, maintain business records, fulfill service obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, or meet legal, tax, accounting, and security requirements. Retention periods vary depending on the nature of the data and the relationship involved.

6. Security measures

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards

We use safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures may include access controls, service-provider protections, monitoring, and administrative processes appropriate for a business website and related communications.

No website, transmission method, or storage environment can be guaranteed as fully secure, so you should avoid sending highly sensitive information through public forms unless specifically requested and protected by a separate secure workflow.

7. Your choices and rights

You can contact us about your information

Depending on applicable law and your location, you may have rights to request access to personal information, ask for corrections, request deletion, object to certain processing, or request information about how data is handled.

To make a request, contact ainvnt@outlook.com. We may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

8. International transfers

Information may be processed where we or our providers operate

Ainvnt works with clients and tools across multiple regions. Information collected through this website may be stored or processed in countries other than your own, subject to applicable safeguards, contractual commitments, and operational requirements.

9. Children's privacy

This website is not directed to children

Our website and services are intended for business and professional audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our website, contact us so we can review and remove it where appropriate.

10. Policy changes

Updates may be made as our website or services evolve

We may revise this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date shown on this page. Continued use of the website after changes are posted indicates acceptance of the revised policy to the extent permitted by law.
