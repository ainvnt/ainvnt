Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about mobile app development.

Should we build native or cross-platform

We assess your requirements and recommend the best approach. Native offers superior performance; cross-platform offers faster development. We'll help you decide.

How long does it take to build an app

Most apps take 3-6 months from discovery to app store launch. Simple apps might be faster; complex apps take longer. We provide detailed timelines during discovery.

Can you handle app store submissions

Absolutely! We manage all app store submissions, optimize listings, create screenshots, and navigate approval processes for iOS and Android.

How do you ensure app security

Security is built-in from day one. Biometric auth, encrypted data, secure APIs, regular security audits, and privacy compliance with GDPR/CCPA.
